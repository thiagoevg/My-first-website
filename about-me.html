How did I become a developer?

Graduated in mechanical engineering at UFRJ, passionate about technology and exact sciences. I feel especially motivated in the face of logical and mathematical challenges, elements that made the computational language quickly become a passion.

I had my first contact with code while I was still in college. There I was introduced to Python in academic subjects such as computation, numeric calculation and mathematical methods, which opened doors previously unknown and provided me the knowledge of this powerful technology for the solution of mathematical and engineering problems.

Since my first contact with code, my admiration and interest for programing has evolved gradually, culminating in the final decision for career change and in an intensive search for qualification in this new area.

I started this new journey by studying on my own, reviewing basic computational concepts through the online course ‘Introduction to Computer Science and Programing in Python’ made available for free on the MIT website. Subsequently I had my first contact with web development through the Bootcamp ‘The Frontend Developer Career Path’ on the Scrimba platform; it was when I fell in love with these technologies.

I am enchanted by the dynamism, power and interactivity of computational languages such as JavaScript, CSS and HTML, and decided to specialize in these technologies, embarking on a 400-hour intensive course at Ironhack/SP. Throughout the course I intend to deepen my knowledges in HTML, JavaScript, CSS, MongoDB, React, Node, Express and Git, in addition to improving personal skills such as discipline and teamwork.
